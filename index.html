Interstellar Odyssey

The ComSSA Hackathon is an annual event hosted by the Computer Science Students Association (ComSSA), the largest computing club at Curtin University, to provide students and young professionals of all disciplines an opportunity to learn, collaborate and solve problems. This year, ComSSA is co-collaborating with the Computer And Security Student Association (CASSA), an association for students studying Computer Science and Cyber Security at Edith Cowan University.

Proudly partnered with the School of Electrical Engineering, Computing and Mathematical Sciences (EECMS), the ComSSA Hackathon, titled Interstellar Odyssey, will be hosted in-person at Bentley campus with a prize pool of $4500.
